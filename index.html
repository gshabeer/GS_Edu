FAQ's
Q. Who should take an Online Course?

Students join our online courses for a variety of reasons and come from diverse backgrounds, professions, and age groups.

Q. How do I select a course?

Visit the course catalog to browse our offerings. In addition to searching by course duration and availability, you can also search by subject to ensure you find a course that reflects your interests.

Q. Are there prerequisites or language requirements?

There are no prerequisites. Our courses are open to students of all backgrounds who are 18 years of age and older. All courses are conducted in English.

Q. I am a high school student, can I join?

Courses are open to all students 18 years or older. If you are under 18 years old, please contact us to discuss further.

Q. Can I take more than one course at a time?

As our courses are offered frequently throughout the year, you can always come back and continue your studies with one or more topics.
